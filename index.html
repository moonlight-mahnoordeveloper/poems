In a mind where echoes softly play,
Where golden dreams and shadows sway,
A world unfolds in pastel hues,
Where truth and fancy gently fuse.

The skies are brushed with whispered lore,
On clouds where waking thoughts implore,
And starlit visions dance in grace,
A fleeting smile on time's embrace.

In corners dim, where daylight sleeps,
A garden blooms, where moonlight creeps,
With petals stitched from silver thread,
And fantasies by moonlight fed.

Where beauty and illusion meet,
