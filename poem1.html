In morning light, the world awakes anew,
With dreams like dew on grass, so fresh and clear,
The sky adorned in shades of dawn’s soft hue,
Life’s canvas waits, its path to reappear



As midday sun climbs high, we chase our goals,
With every step, the moments weave and blend,
Our laughter rings, our hopes and fears unfold,
In vibrant hues, the day begins to end



As twilight casts its gentle, fading glow,
Reflections dance on waves of time and space,
We find our peace in memories that flow,
And cherish every curve upon life’s face.



With stars above, our thoughts begin to rest,
In dreams we journey far beyond the night,
Each day a gift, each moment truly blessed,
In life’s grand dance, we find our hearts’ delight.
